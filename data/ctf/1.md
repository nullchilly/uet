## Hành vi của virus có 3 phần:
  - how to infection (cách thức lan truyền)
  - trigger condition
  - malicious behavior

## Virus classifications:
  ### By target
    - boot
    - file infector
    - macro virus
    - multiparties
  ### By strategy
    - encrypted virus
    - stealth (hide from antivirus)
    - polymorphic virus
